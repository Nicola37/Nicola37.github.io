About Me
Hello all, and welcome to my website. My name is Nicola Frachesen. Given my degree in computer science with a focus on design, and a passion for both the written and spoken word, I hope to make technology and other services accessible and inviting to those who need it most.

On a more personal note, I enjoy reading, hiking, video games, international politics, and being intensely curious about pretty much everything.
My Work
Dying Dreams
My personal senior project, Dying Dreams is a video game with no video; a completely audio-based role-playing game designed to be inclusive of the visually impaired, and engaging for all. It was made in Unity, is 3 to 4 hours long, and is fully voiced. My paper on it can be found here.
Donator
Group project from my UX Design class made for a client from the Royal Bank of Canada. We designed, wireframed, and presented an app for donating to those in need. It was created using Invision. Our presentation poster can be found here.
PK Yonge EOC
As the group project for a Software Engineering class, we created an online practice test web app for students at PK Yonge High School. It was created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and the MEAN stack framework. More information can be found here.

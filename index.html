About Me
Hello all, and welcome to my website. My name is Nicola Frachesen and I'm a designer, writer, and programmer with a focus on user experience, accessibility, and communication. Thus, I hope to make technology and other services impactful and inviting to those who need it most. I'm currently performing contract work on the VIBES project for Deep Designs, LLC, which supports children's social and emotional learning.

On a more personal note, I enjoy reading, hiking, video games, unusual foods, and being intensely curious about pretty much everything.
My Work
VIBES
VIBES is a software project aimed at providing personalized assistance to middle school-aged students in developing their emotional well-being and interpersonal skills. The platform will also provide parents and educators the ability to better support their children's social-emotional learning. As a contractor on the project, my contributions have been in student-side interaction and UX design, the ideation and writing of interactive scenarios, and the development of project prototypes. More information can be found here.
Dying Dreams
My personal senior project, Dying Dreams is a video game with no video; a completely audio-based role-playing game designed to be inclusive of the visually impaired and engaging for all. It was made using Unity and 3D audio technology, and is fully voiced. My paper on it can be found here.
Donator
Group project from my UX Design class made for a client from the Royal Bank of Canada. Following research, interviews, and generating Personas, we wireframed and prototyped an app for donating to those in need, primarily using Invision. Our presentation can be found here.
PK Yonge EOC
As the group project for a Software Engineering class, we created an online practice test web app for students at PK Yonge High School. It was created with HTML, CSS, JavaScript and the MEAN stack framework. More information can be found here.
